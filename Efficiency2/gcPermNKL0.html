The MPermNKL Benchmark

The GCBench benchmark was designed to be representative of real applications.

The MPermNKL benchmark was designed to be a near-worst-case for generational garbage collection.

There are three outliers, two for gcc and one for Larceny, showing those systems to be much faster than Java. The most likely explanation for those outliers is that they were run or reported incorrectly.
