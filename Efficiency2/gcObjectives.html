Learning Objectives

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to

  • give a truthful answer when someone asks you which is faster: garbage collection or explicit deallocation
  • cite one area of performance in which explicit deallocation often has an advantage over garbage collection
  • identify three reasons why garbage collection often runs faster than explicit deallocation
  • identify several forms of special pleading often employed by programmers who want you to believe explicit deallocation is faster than garbage collection
