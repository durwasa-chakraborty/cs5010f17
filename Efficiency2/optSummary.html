Summary

  • Optimizing compilers save programmers from worrying about the micro-efficiency of their programs.
  • Optimizing compilers usually improve the running time of a program by some small constant factor.
  • In a few cases, compiler optimization can improve the asymptotic running time or asymptotic space efficiency of a program.
  • Standard optimizations include:
    • elimination of redundant safety checks
    • reduction in strength
    • common subexpression elimination
