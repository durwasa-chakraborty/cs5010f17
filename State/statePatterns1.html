Patterns of Communication Between Objects

In this module, we will consider objects with mutable state. We sometimes refer to mutable objects as stateful objects.

Objects can communicate in two basic ways: pull and push.

In the functional paradigm, we used pull-style communication: Active objects (functions) ask other objects (functions and data structures) to give them information they need.

With stateful objects, some patterns of push-style communication become easier.

Publish-subscribe is a common pattern of push-style communication.

Delegates are a refinement of publish-subscribe.
