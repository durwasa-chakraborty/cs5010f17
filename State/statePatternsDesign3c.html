Push Model: What Happens

  • b1.intersects(b2)
  • b1 sends its data to b2.
  • b2 does the arithmetic.

In this design, b2 doesn't know who is asking it to do the arithmetic.

This is sometimes called double dispatch. It shows up often in object-oriented programming.

A related pattern is called the visitor pattern (which is mostly unrelated to the functional visitor pattern, which is more important). The visitor pattern involves a double dispatch when one of the structures is itemization data.
